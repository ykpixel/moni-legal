Frequently asked questions

How do I add a transaction?

Tap the capture button and either snap a photo of a receipt, record a short voice note describing the expense, or enter it manually. You can also import a bank statement (see below).

Can I import my bank statement?

Yes. Go to Settings → Import Data → Import Bank Statement and pick an OFX/QFX or CSV file exported from your bank. For CSV files you'll map the columns once. Importing is processed entirely on your device — no AI, and your statement never leaves your phone.

Where is my data stored? Is it private?

Your transactions, wallets, and budgets are stored locally on your device. We don't keep them on a server. When you scan a receipt or record a voice note, only that single image/audio clip is sent for AI processing and is not retained afterwards. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I back up or move my data to a new phone?

Use the in-app export options under Settings to export your data, then keep that file somewhere safe (cloud drive, email). Uninstalling the app or wiping the device removes the local data, so export before you switch phones.

What are the AI capture limits?

To keep the app sustainable, AI receipt scans and voice logs are limited to a set number per month. Manual entry and bank-statement import are always unlimited. If you reach the limit, it resets at the start of the next month.

The app didn't read my receipt / voice correctly.

Try a clearer, well-lit photo of the full receipt, or speak the amount and what it was for clearly (e.g. "spent 12 dollars on coffee"). You can always edit any detail before saving, or add the transaction manually.

How do I delete my data or account?

Delete individual items in the app at any time, or remove everything via Settings → Clear Local Data; uninstalling also removes local data. To delete an email account or server-side identifier, email us at the address above.
