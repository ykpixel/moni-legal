Privacy Policy

Moni — AI-Powered Expense Management

Effective date: 7 June 2026  ·  Last updated: 7 June 2026

The short version: Moni stores your financial data on your device. We don't run servers that hold your transactions, and we never sell your data. The only time information leaves your phone is when you choose to scan a receipt or record a voice note — that single image or audio clip is sent for AI processing and is not stored afterwards.

1. Who we are

Moni ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal expense-management application. For any privacy questions, contact us at support@ykpixel.com.

2. Data stored on your device

By design, Moni is offline-first. The following is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us:

  • Transactions (amounts, dates, merchants, categories, notes, tags).
  • Wallets, budgets, and recurring-transaction settings.
  • App preferences (currency, etc.).
  • Receipt photos you capture are saved to your device's photo gallery.

If you uninstall the app or wipe your device, this local data is removed by the operating system. Use the in-app export feature to keep your own backup.

3. Device permissions

  • Camera — only to photograph receipts you choose to scan.
  • Microphone — only while you are recording a voice entry.
  • Photos / storage — to save receipt images and to import statement files you select.
  • Notifications — to alert you when a budget threshold is reached.

We do not access your camera, microphone, photos, or files in the background or without an explicit action by you.

4. AI processing (receipt & voice capture)

When you scan a receipt or record a voice note, that specific image or audio clip is sent — over an encrypted connection, through our processing service — to OpenAI, which extracts the transaction details (merchant, amount, category, etc.) and returns them to your device.

  • Only the media you explicitly capture for this purpose is sent.
  • The extracted result is stored locally on your device, under your control.
  • We do not retain the image or audio on our servers after processing.
  • OpenAI processes the data as a service provider; per OpenAI's API policy, API data is not used to train their models.

If you only ever enter transactions manually or import statement files, no data is sent for AI processing. Bank-statement import (OFX/QFX/CSV) is parsed entirely on your device with no AI and no network call.

5. Authentication and processing infrastructure

We use Supabase to (a) issue an anonymous account identifier so the app can function, and (b) operate the secure function that relays your capture to OpenAI. This anonymous identifier is not linked to your name and is used to apply fair-use limits on AI processing. If you choose to create an account with an email address, that email is stored to let you sign back in.

6. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to anyone.
  • We do not show third-party advertising.
  • We do not connect to your bank or access your bank credentials.
  • We do not track your location.

7. Data retention & deletion

Your financial data lives on your device; you can delete any item, or all of it, at any time from within the app, and uninstalling removes it entirely. To delete an account you created (email) or any associated server-side identifier, email support@ykpixel.com and we will remove it.

8. Children

Moni is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. the EU/EEA under GDPR, or California under CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Because most of your data is stored only on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly in the app. For anything else, contact us.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, through an in-app notice.
